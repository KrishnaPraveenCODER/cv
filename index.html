Gardening tips for Beginners

Starting to build a garden can be both exciting and daughting.It all starts with a sad patch of grass and you keep growing, it transforms int a beautiful garden with flowers, fruits, trees, veggis etc.
Garden beautifies your homeand is an excellent way to spend your time after work.

Some tips for Gardening:

1.Pickup a right spot for gardining.


2.Pick a type of plant you want to grow.


3.Use the basic Gardening tools.


4.Work on soil.

Plants always benefits from the nutrient rich garden soil. take your first step by examing the texture of the soil, it should be shovelled and crumble in our hands.
If the texture of the soil is hard then it is difficult to grow the plants.
The nutrients required for the plants growth are :

  • Soil derived macronutrients
    1. Nitrogen
    2. Phosphorous
    3. Potassium
    4. Sulphur
    5. Calcium
    6. Magnesium
  • soil derived micronutrients
    1. Boron
    2. Chlorin
    3. Copper
    4. Iron
    5. Magnesium
    6. Molybdenum
    7. Nickel
    8. Zinc
